In this cooking contest, the awards are distributed based on the scores received by the dishes prepared by the contestants:

This prestigious award is bestowed upon the chef who crafts a menu comprising the three dishes that garner the highest scores from all attendees. It recognizes exceptional culinary skill and creativity in curating a winning combination of flavors and presentations.

The second prize is awarded to the contestant whose menu earns the second-highest overall score from the attendees. This accolade acknowledges culinary excellence and innovation, highlighting the outstanding quality of the dishes presented.

The third prize is granted to the individual who achieves the highest score specifically for their dessert creation. This award celebrates mastery in the art of dessert-making, showcasing delectable flavors, textures, and presentation techniques that captivate the taste buds and leave a lasting impression.

Each prize category represents a distinct aspect of culinary expertise and achievement, reflecting the diverse talents and creativity showcased in the competition.

The champion of this esteemed cooking competition will not only claim ultimate bragging rights, but also walk away with a truly transformative prize package. Await a lucrative €10,000 cash reward to fuel your culinary dreams, along with the prestigious honor of having your recipes published in a dedicated cookbook!

In second at this competition is no small feat! The runner-up will be rewarded with a substantial €5,000 cash prize to invest in further culinary endeavors. But that's not all! You'll also receive a top-of-the-line knife kit, the essential tools for any aspiring chef.

As a third-place winner, you'll receive a gratifying €1,000 cash prize, a welcome boost to explore new culinary ventures or invest in your kitchen.
But the real treat comes in the form of an unforgettable dining experience for two at a prestigious Michelin-starred restaurant! Immerse yourselves in the artistry of world-class chefs, savor innovative dishes, and gain inspiration from the pinnacle of culinary excellence.
